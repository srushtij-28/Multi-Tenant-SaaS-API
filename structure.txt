🧠 Why This Is Important

Without Multi-Tenancy:
Customer A → Separate App
Customer B → Separate App
Customer C → Separate App

High infrastructure cost and difficult maintenance.
With Multi-Tenancy:

              SaaS Application
                     │
     ┌───────────────┼───────────────┐
     ▼               ▼               ▼
 Tenant A       Tenant B       Tenant C
   Data            Data            Data

Benefits:
✅ Shared infrastructure
✅ Lower deployment cost
✅ Customer data isolation
✅ Easier maintenance
✅ Scalable SaaS architecture
